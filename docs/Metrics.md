# Product Metrics

## North Star Metric

**Daily Quiz Completion Rate**

Definition:
Percentage of users who complete the daily quiz after starting it.

Formula:
Completed Quizzes / Started Quizzes × 100

Target:
> 85%

---

## Primary Metrics

### Daily Active Users (DAU)

Tracks the number of users opening the app daily.

Target:
+20%

---

### Quiz Participation Rate

Users who attempt today's quiz.

Target:
65%

---

### Average Quiz Score

Measures learning effectiveness.

Target:
70%+

---

### Average Time to Complete Quiz

Target:
8–10 minutes

---

### Weekly Retention

Percentage of users returning each week.

Target:
+15%

---

### Average Streak Length

Measures consistency.

Target:
12+ days

---
